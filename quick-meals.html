Ingredients

  • 500g strawberries, sliced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add the eggs and butter, and mix well.
  4. Gradually add the milk until the batter is smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking dish.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown.
