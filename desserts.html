Ingredients

  • 500g strawberries, sliced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add the eggs and butter, and mix well.
  4. Gradually add the milk until the batter is smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking dish.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown.
110g (1/2 cup) caster sugar 170g (2/3 cup) mascarpone 2 tsp vanilla essence 2 egg whites Strawberries, halved, extra, to serve Step 1 Place the 500g strawberries, sliced with 1 tablespoon of the caster sugar in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until the sugar dissolves and strawberries soften. Remove from the heat. Transfer to a heatproof bowl and place in the freezer for 10 minutes to cool. Step 2 Place the 170g (2/3 cup) mascarpone, strawberry mixture and 2 tsp vanilla essence in a bowl. Use a large spoon to fold until just combined (do not over-mix). Step 3 Use electric beaters to beat the 2 egg whites in a separate bowl until soft peaks form. Gradually add the remaining sugar until thick and glossy. Use a large metal spoon to gently fold the egg white mixture into the mascarpone mixture until just combined. Spoon among serving glasses. Decorate with the Strawberries, halved, extra, to serve to serve.
